## Ship System (Backlog)

Priority: Medium-High
**Goal:** Full ship customization, purchasing, and equipment system.

### Ship Purchase & Customization
- Buy new ship hulls at stations (different stats: hull, speed, cargo, weapon slots)
- Ship skins / paint jobs
- Named ship (player can name their ship)

### Equipment Slots
- Weapon slots (primary, secondary)
- Utility slots (scanner, shields, fuel tank, cargo expander)
- Engine slot (affects speed, maneuverability)

### Scanner Equipment
- Level 1 Scanner: minimap shows static objects only (current behavior)
- Level 2 Scanner: minimap shows NPC ships in radius
- Level 3 Scanner: shows all moving objects + zone resource density overlay
- Scanner range upgrades unlock more minimap detail

### New Weapons
- Missile launcher (slow, high damage, limited ammo)
- Area blast (short range, hits multiple)
- Mining laser upgrade (faster mining + minor combat)

### Shield System (already in backlog separately)
- Equipment slot item, not captain perk
